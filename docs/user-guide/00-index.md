# OpenSMPSDeck User Guide

OpenSMPSDeck is a standalone SMPS-native music tracker for composing YM2612 FM and SN76489 PSG music targeting the Sega Mega Drive / Genesis sound hardware. It produces SMPS-compatible output that can be injected directly into Sonic ROMs or played by the OpenGGF sonic-engine. What you compose in the tracker is exactly what exports -- the internal model is SMPS bytecode.

## Where to Start

- **New to trackers** -- Start with [Quick Start](01-quick-start.md) to make your first sound, then follow [Tutorial: Your First Song](02-tutorial-first-song.md) for a full walkthrough.
- **Experienced tracker user** -- Read the [Interface Overview](03-interface-overview.md) to get oriented, then jump to whichever reference chapter you need.
- **ROM hacker / Sonic modder** -- Head straight to [Importing](10-importing.md) to bring in existing music and [SMPS Modes](12-smps-modes.md) to understand driver differences.
